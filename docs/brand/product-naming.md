# Gastos — Product Naming

_Last updated: 2026-04-19_

## Brand Architecture

**Gastos** is the parent brand. It houses a family of personal-finance products that share brand values (calm, competent, dark-mode-first, descriptive-not-prescriptive) but serve different surfaces and jobs-to-be-done.

### Product family

| Product name | Surface | Job-to-be-done |
|---|---|---|
| **Gastos Spending Tracker** (iOS) | Mobile app | Quick in-the-moment expense capture (text, photo, audio) |
| **Gastos Spending Tracker** (Telegram bot) | Chat | Quick in-the-moment expense capture via chat |
| **Gastos Together** | Web app (this repo) | Consolidate bank/credit-card statements across multiple accounts and household members into one clean, deduped, shared view |

## Naming Rationale — "Gastos Together"

"Together" signals the two axes this app consolidates across:

1. **Across accounts** — multiple banks, multiple cards, multiple statement formats unified in one place.
2. **Across household members** — spouses/partners who share finances use this app jointly; it is the shared source of truth, not a single person's log.

The capture-focused siblings ("Spending Tracker") are for one person, one moment. **Gastos Together** is where that individual activity — plus bulk statement history — comes together at the household level.

## Usage

- **Primary product name (formal):** Gastos Together
- **Short form (in-app, headers):** Gastos Together, or "Together" when the parent-brand context is already established
- **Reference in docs/specs:** use "Gastos Together" on first mention per document, then "the app" or short form is fine
